Our Programs


English as a Second Language


This program will teach you how to read, write, speak and listen to English. Both children and adult students learn English, the benefit of ESL is that they can communicate with native-speaking friends and family within the community while reaching out to people who communicate only in English. ESL can bridge the gap between their families and the rest of the world. Classes are both offered for kids and adults no matter the level.

Youth Education


Children are our future and it's important for them to be educated. We serve children and youth of all ages. We focus on long-term relationshi​ps with our youth and families. We will educate and provide support on the growth and development. The goal is to assist in the development of one's talent and potential to become successful. Educational programs can support social, emotional, cognitive, and academic development, reduce risky behaviors, promote physical health, and provide a safe and supportive environment for children and youth.

Legal Assistance


In partnership with the Charlotte Center for Legal Advocacy, Hogar is now offering free legal advice and legal representation with top of the line immigration lawyers. We assist in the following: Fighting againist deportation, starting the path toward citizenship, and gaining legal status to work in the United States with the H-1B visa program.

Housing


Here at Hogar we aim to provide safe, secure, and affordable housing. We believe that housing is a human right. Housing affects almost everything. Research shows that not having a stable home can have enduring effects on families’ ability to obtain basic necessities (e.g., food, clothing, and medicine). Housing instability can lead to frequent school moves, and low test scores among children.
