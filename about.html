About Us

Hello! We are Hogar. Here at Hogar we provide ESL classes for immigrants & refugees. Our Program Manager is none other than Sam Rivas Ramirez, he handles all the program details & works along side our Education Director - Seth Mulia. Mr. Mulia is in charge of our ESL program & he's our recruiter for ESL tutor's. Axel Vargas-Jimenes is our Marketing director, he manages all our ad campaigns & social medias. Isaac Santos is our Financial Officer, he manages our donation proceeds & makes sure they go to the right departments. Last but definitely not least we have out CEO the one & only Jacky Mendoza. Together we will make a difference & impact the lives of refugees & immigrants.
